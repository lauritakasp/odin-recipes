Lasagna

this is a classical lasagna recipe that is easy to follow

Ingredients:

  • lasagna sheets
  • mozarella
  • mornay sauce
  • beef or any other meat of your choice
  • canned tomatoes
  • garlic

Steps

  1. cut garlic and saute it on the pan
  2. add beef and brown it
  3. add canned tomatoes and simmer everything for 30 mins.
  4. turn on the oven to 200C
  5. place lasagna sheets with sauce and layer it
  6. bake and eat
