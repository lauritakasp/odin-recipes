Pasta Bolognese

Pasta bolognese is a typical italian dish. it normally consists of spaghetti and tomato sauce with meat

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Beef
  • canned tomatoes
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. boil water, add salt
  2. brown beef
  3. at canned tomatoes to the beef
  4. add spaghetti to the water
  5. serve
